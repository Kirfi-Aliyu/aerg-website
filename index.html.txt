<!-- About -->
<section id="about">
  <h2>About the AERG Programme</h2>
  <p>The AERG Programme is aligned with SDG4 and designed to reduce the number of out-of-school children in Nigeria. It is structured around four core pillars:</p>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Attract:</strong> Drive awareness and mobilization campaigns in communities</li>
    <li><strong>Enroll:</strong> Streamline and simplify enrollment with locally adapted processes</li>
    <li><strong>Retain:</strong> Provide learner welfare support including nutrition and health</li>
    <li><strong>Graduate:</strong> Deliver academic and vocational skills to ensure educational continuity</li>
  </ul>
</section>

<!-- How It Works -->
<section id="how">
  <h2>How It Works</h2>
  <ol>
    <li>Conduct community assessments and awareness outreach</li>
    <li>Register and onboard learners with starter kits</li>
    <li>Offer inclusive retention services such as school meals and medical aid</li>
    <li>Facilitate structured learning with monitoring tools</li>
    <li>Celebrate graduations with certification and media visibility</li>
  </ol>
</section>
